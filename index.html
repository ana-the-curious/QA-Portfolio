About Me

My approach to quality

I believe quality is not a phase — it's a mindset that runs through every stage of the SDLC. I enjoy building testing processes from the ground up, asking the right questions early, and making risk visible to stakeholders before it becomes a production incident. My background spans E-commerce, EdTech, Travel, SaaS, and Gamedev, which has taught me to adapt quickly to new domains and contexts.

People, curiosity, learning, impact, and clarity. I care about the end user and about the teams I work with.
